How Immediate Painters Works

A managed, supervised process for painting projects.

For Clients

Clients request a managed painting project through Immediate Painters. Project scope, timing, and requirements are reviewed before proceeding.

For Painters

Independent painting contractors may be engaged by Immediate Painters for specific projects by invitation. Contractors remain independent and responsible for their own business obligations.

Supervision & Coordination

Immediate Painters may assist with coordination, communication, and independent project oversight where agreed.

Quotes, Agreements & Payments

Quotes are requested and reviewed as part of the managed project process. Payments and contractual arrangements are agreed directly between clients and contractors unless otherwise specified in writing.
