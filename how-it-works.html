How Immediate Painters Works

A simple, supervised process for painters and clients.

For Painters

Create a profile, submit quotes, and work under supervision if required. Painters may be independent contractors or part of supervised teams.

For Clients

Post your painting job, receive clear quotes, approve work, and track progress with confidence.

Supervision & Compliance

Quotes may be reviewed by experienced supervisors to ensure clarity, pricing transparency, and basic NZ compliance expectations.

Payments & Agreements

Immediate Painters facilitates communication and structure only. Payments and contracts are agreed directly between painters and clients.
